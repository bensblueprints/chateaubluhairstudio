Our Story

A Boutique Experience

Established in Winter 2016, Chateau Blu Hair Studio was born from a passion for creating an intimate, luxurious space where every client feels like family. Nestled in the heart of Newtown Square, our boutique studio specializes in dimensional color, lived-in balayage, precision cutting, and special occasion styling.


We use only the highest quality professional products and stay at the forefront of technique and trend. Whether you're looking for a subtle refresh or a complete transformation, our experienced team brings artistry and care to every appointment.
